Big Brekky

Description

This curried tofu on toast recipe contains a good source of carbs, protein, and healthy fats, coupled with delicious taste!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 packet tofu
  • curry powder
  • splash soy milk
  • half avocado
  • 2 slices multi-grain bread
  1. Toast bread
  2. Crush avocado and spread on toast
  3. Crush tofu and place in pan
  4. Add curry powder and splash of milk
  5. Scramble
  6. Place tofu on toast
  7. Enjoy!
