Nutrition Bowl

Description

This nutrition bowl recipe contains a good source of carbs, protein, and healthy fats, coupled with delicious taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 can lentils
  • 1 jar curry paste
  • 1 cup mixed winter vegetables
  1. Pour all ingredients into pan
  2. Stir
  3. Enjoy!
