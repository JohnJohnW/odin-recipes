Oatmeal

Description

This oatmeal recipe contains a good source of carbs, protein, and healthy fats, coupled with delicious taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole banana
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  1. Pour oats into bowl
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Microwave for 1 min
  4. Add scoop of protein powder
  5. Stir
  6. Cut banana into slices
  7. Top oats with banana slices
  8. Enjoy!
